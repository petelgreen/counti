# Product Context

We are building an MVP calorie tracking app.

Main value:
- Users can log what they ate quickly
- Users can type food descriptions manually
- Users can upload food images
- The app estimates calories using AI
- Users can review and edit the detected meal items
- The app stores meals in a daily log

MVP priorities:
- fast logging
- understandable AI output
- editable results
- daily calorie total

Out of scope for initial MVP:
- barcode scanning
- social features
- meal plans
- nutritionist dashboards
- advanced analytics
